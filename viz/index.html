How to read this

  • Tokens are the words in the sentence.
  • Arcs show attention: which word a token looks at.
  • Thicker arcs mean stronger attention weight.
  • Attention heads learn different patterns (syntax, nearby words, boundaries).
  • Use Top-K to show only the strongest connections.
  • Press Play to cycle through attention heads.
